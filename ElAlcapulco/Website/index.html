Family Mexican

The Acapulco Restaurant is a family ran business with a mission to bring Mexican culture and food to Knoxville. We have real authentic recipes in every dish we serve, ensuring that you are being served real authentic tastes from the ones in Acapulco. We have a variety of selections to pick from for the whole family to eat!!!
